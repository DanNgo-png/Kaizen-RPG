Company Origins

Choose your company's starting background. Higher difficulty yields rarer loot and higher renown.

Recommended

The Sellswords

The classic experience. Complete tasks to earn Gold and XP. Failed focus sessions result in minor Fatigue, but no permanent injuries to your squad.

  • Standard Loot Drops
  • Flexible Focus permitted
  • No Permadeath

Lone Wolf

You start with one elite Mercenary. All Focus Time goes to their stats. **Permadeath Enabled:** If you fail 3 focus sessions in a row, the mercenary retires and you lose their gear.

  • +50% XP Multiplier
  • High Risk: Permadeath
  • Exclusive Legendary Gear

The Great War

A season-based mode. Contribute Focus Hours to a global or faction-based goal. Defend your "stronghold" by completing your daily Kanban board.

  • Weekly Faction Rewards
  • Strategic Base Building
  • Leaderboard Access

Ironman Patrol

A "Roguelite" productivity mode. Every focus session is a random encounter. Fail a timer, and the "run" ends, resetting your daily gold multiplier.

  • Random Stat Buffs
  • Streak-only progression
  • Quick 25m sessions
